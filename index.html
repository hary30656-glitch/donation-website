About Us

We uplift communities through generosity and action, where kindness sparks hope in every soul.

Kindred Flame was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to ignite hope in communities across Nepal. We believe that every act of kindness, no matter how small, has the power to create lasting change. Through our dedicated team of volunteers and generous donors, we've touched thousands of lives across education, health, and emergency relief.
